M.S. Cognitive Neuroscience Ph.D. candidate, University of California Irvine From Los Angeles

“I had a vague idea what a castle looked like in terms of the base. The top and towers were completely new to me; I'd always heard of towers and castles but didn't imagine the towers were a part of the castle. I thought the third dimension looked like a triangle like houses do. I also didn't know bridges were attached to castles.”
